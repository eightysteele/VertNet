Did you know? The FishNet, HerpNET, MaNIS, and ORNIS communities have joined forces under the new VertNet Project with support from the National Science Foundation! It's a collaboration between the University of California at Berkeley, the University of Colorado at Boulder, the University of Kansas Center for Research, and Tulane University.

So what is VertNet? With a focus on users, performance, and outreach, VertNet will provide open web access to data from a sustainable cloud-based infrastructure with new capabilities for super-fast discovery and visualization. By integrating with several existing projects and applications, we hope to transform the use of vertebrate biodiversity data for cross-disciplinary research, conservation, and policy-making.

We're just getting started with the project, but you can still search for data using the original portals below. Stay tuned!
